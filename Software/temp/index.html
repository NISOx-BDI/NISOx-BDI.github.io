To keep and maintain resources to support SnPM13 we need to know something about the demographics of SnPM13 user base, in terms of location, modality, and previous SnPM13 usage. Therefore, prior to downloading SnPM13 software, please complete the following registration form:

  • Please supply at least your name, institution and location.
  • This information is for demographic purposes only.
    No individual details will be released.
  • Your email address (if supplied) will not be added to any list or publicly accessible file.
    (The SPM email discussion list will remain the forum for discussions and announcements regarding SnPM13.)
    Only in exceptional circumstances will we contact you directly regarding the software you are about to download.
  • It would be helpful for us if you could indicate the imaging modalities you use, how many people will use the software you are downloading, and which (if any) versions of the SnPM13 software you have previously used.
  • Any additional comments will be emailed directly to the SnPM13 authors.
  • Posting the form will take you to a page giving you the URL's for downloading the requested SnPM13 software.
