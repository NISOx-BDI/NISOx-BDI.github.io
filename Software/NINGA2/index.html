Introduction

NINGA is a toolbox for heritability inference and association inference controlling for heritability. The Matlab-Based toolbox designed for spm8, spm 12 or FSL and is currently in Beta testing. To download NINGA click here.

Current features include:
  • Fast and powerful voxel-wise heritability analysis.
  • Covariate inference in presence of nuisance kinship matrix.
  • Fast and powerful Genome-wide association analysis for related and unrelated individuals.
  • Can provide Family-wise corrected pvalues for spatial statistics, i.e., cluster size or TFCE statistics.
