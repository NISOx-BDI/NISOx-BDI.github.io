Download Registration Form

To keep and maintain resources to support we need to know something about the demographics of user base, in terms of location, modality, and previous usage. Therefore, prior to downloading software, please complete the following registration form:

  • Please supply at least your name, institution and location.
  • This information is for demographic purposes only.
    No individual details will be released.
  • Your email address (if supplied) will not be added to any list or publicly accessible file.
    (The SPM email discussion list will remain the forum for discussions and announcements regarding .)
    Only in exceptional circumstances will we contact you directly regarding the software you are about to download.
  • It would be helpful for us if you could indicate the imaging modalities you use, how many people will use the software you are downloading, and which (if any) versions of the software you have previously used.
  • Any additional comments will be emailed directly to the authors.
  • Posting the form will take you to a page giving you the URL's for downloading the requested software.

Privacy statement
This form is anonymous. No data which personally identifies you is collected on the form, and the data you provide is used solely to help us improve the delivery of our courses.
