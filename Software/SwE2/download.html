To keep and maintain resources to support SwE we need to know something about the demographics of SwE user base, in terms of location, modality, and previous SwE usage. Therefore, prior to downloading SwE software, please complete the following registration form:

  • Please supply at least your name, institution and location.
  • This information is for demographic purposes only.
    No individual details will be released.
  • Your email address (if supplied) will not be added to any list or publicly accessible file.
    (The SPM email discussion list will remain the forum for discussions and announcements regarding SwE.)
    Only in exceptional circumstances will we contact you directly regarding the software you are about to download.
  • It would be helpful for us if you could indicate the imaging modalities you use, how many people will use the software you are downloading, and which (if any) versions of the SwE software you have previously used.
  • Any additional comments will be emailed directly to the SwE authors.
  • Posting the form will take you to a page giving you the URL's for downloading the requested SwE software.
