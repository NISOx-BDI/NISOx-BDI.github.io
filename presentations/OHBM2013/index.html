This collection organizes a number of different posters and talks from NISOx and NISOx collaborators at the 2013 Organization for Human Brain Mapping (OHBM) in Seattle, 16-20 June.

Talks

This section lists OHBM 2013 standalone talks by NISOx and NISOx collaborators.
  • Voxel-wise and Cluster-based Heritability Inference for fMRI Data
    Authors: Xu Chen, Gabriella Blokland, Lachlan Strike, and Thomas Nichols. Selected for oral presentation and first author was travel award winner. doi:10.6084/m9.figshare.5484373.v1
  • Analysis of Multiple Sclerosis Lesions via Spatially Varying Coefficients
    Authors: Tian Ge, Nicole Muller-Lenke, Kerstin Bendfeldt, Thomas Nichols, and Timothy D. Johnson. Selected for oral presentation and first author was travel award winner. doi:10.6084/m9.figshare.5484379.v1
  • Overview of Meta-Analysis Approaches
    Author: Thomas Nichols. Neuroimaging Meta-Analysis. doi:10.6084/m9.figshare.5484382.v1
