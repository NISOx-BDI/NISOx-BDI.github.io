Welcome to the homepage of the NeuroImaging Statistics Oxford (NISOx) research group lead by Professor Thomas Nichols's at the Oxford Big Data Institute.

The NISOx group performs a wide variety of Neuroimaging research which focuses on developing Neuroimaging inference methods for large scale, population-size fMRI studies. Current areas of interest in which members of NISOx are involved in include Threshold-Free Cluster Enhancement (TFCE), Effective Connectivity, Coordinate-Based and Image-Based Meta-Analysis, to name a few.

For a full list of research publications presented by the NISOx group please see the publications page.
